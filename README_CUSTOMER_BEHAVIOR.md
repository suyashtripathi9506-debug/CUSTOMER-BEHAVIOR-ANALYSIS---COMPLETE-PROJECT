## 📊 KEY METRICS CALCULATED

- **RFM Analysis:** Recency, Frequency, Monetary value segmentation
- **Churn Risk:** Low/Medium/High/Critical customer attrition prediction
- **CLV (Customer Lifetime Value):** 3-year revenue projection
- **Engagement Score:** Composite metric (0-100 scale)
- **Purchase Frequency:** Transactions per month
- **Value Segment:** Star Customer / Big Spender / Frequent Buyer / Development
- **Conversion Rate:** Customer conversion from engagement to purchase
- **Health Score:** Overall customer relationship strength
